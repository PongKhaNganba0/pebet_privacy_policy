Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect a variety of information from and about you when you use the Services. This includes:

Information you provide to us directly: We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and phone number. We may also collect other information that you choose to share with us, such as photos or videos.

Information we collect automatically: When you use the Services, we may collect certain information automatically, such as the type of device you are using, your IP address, and information about your device's operating system and browser. We may also collect information about your location, as well as information about your use of the Services, such as the pages you visit and the actions you take within the App.

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the Services, as well as to personalize your experience. We may also use the information for research and analytics purposes, to communicate with you about the Services or other products and services we think may be of interest to you, and for other business purposes.

Sharing of Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

With your consent: We may share your information with third parties when you have given us your consent to do so.

With service providers: We may share your information with service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as hosting or maintaining the Services, analyzing data, or providing customer support. These service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose.

For legal reasons: We may share your information if we believe it is necessary to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request. We may also share your information if we believe it is necessary to protect the safety, rights, or property of PEBET, our users, or the public.

In the event of a merger or acquisition: If PEBET is acquired by or merged with another company, we may share your information with that company in order to continue providing the Services.

Children's Privacy

The Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

It is important to keep in mind that children's online safety is the responsibility of their parents or guardians. It is recommended that parents or guardians closely supervise their children's use of the internet and mobile apps, including the PEBET app.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child under the age of 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us at click here.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. However, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized parties will never be able to defeat those measures or use your personal information for improper purposes.

If you have any concerns about the security of your personal information, please contact us at click here. We will take reasonable steps to address any concerns you may have.
