Privacy Policy for Pebet Calendar
Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Pebet ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pebet Calendar mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use
Pebet Calendar is designed to be used without requiring you to create an account, register, or provide any directly identifiable personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

However, we use third-party services to monitor and analyze the performance of our App. These services may collect certain non-personally identifiable information automatically.

A. Automatically Collected Information
To help us improve the App, troubleshoot bugs, and understand user engagement, we use third-party services provided by Google:

Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data (e.g., app opens, session duration, screen views, and user interactions within the app). This helps us understand which features are most popular and how users interact with the calendar.
Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash logs, stack traces, and device performance information when the App experiences a crash or error. This data is entirely anonymous and is used solely to identify and fix technical issues.

B. Device Information
The service providers may automatically collect details about the device you use to access the App, such as:

Device model and manufacturer
Operating system version (iOS or Android)
Unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
IP address (anonymized/truncated)

2. Permissions
Pebet Calendar may request standard device permissions to function correctly (e.g., network access to initialize Firebase). We do not access sensitive permissions such as your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or local photo gallery.

3. Third-Party Services
We integrate third-party SDKs that have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data:

Google Play Services: Google Play Services Privacy Policy
Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): Privacy and Security in Firebase

4. Data Retention and Deletion
Since we do not collect or store personal data (like names or emails) on our own servers, we do not retain any personal information. Analytical data collected by Firebase is stored securely by Google in accordance with Firebase's data retention policies.

5. Children's Privacy
Our App is safe for all age groups and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take steps to remove it.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
